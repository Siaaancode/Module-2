How to Use Budget Birdie

Budget Birdie is a simple and intuitive tool that helps you manage your finances effectively. To get started, simply enter your income and expenses into the calculator. Budget Birdie will provide you with a clear overview of your financial situation, allowing you to make informed decisions about your spending and saving habits.

1. Gather your financial information: Monthly incomes and expenses. As well as any other relevant financial details, such as 1 off expenses, credit card debts, etc.

2. Enter your information into the calculator and click "Submit" to see where you stand financially.

3. Review your total and adjust your spending habits to save or budget accordingly.
